Game Design Fundamentals

This page is for aspiring tabletop game designers to flex their creative muscles as they lay the groundwork for their first game.

These are some important points to consider when you first start working on a new design:

  • What kind of game do you want to make?
  • Who is your target audience?
  • What type of experience do you want to create for the player?
  • Do you want to publish your game?
